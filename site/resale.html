How resale works

No booking fee, no pickup fee, no work on your end.

It starts with an intake request. From there the work is mine to carry.

Free pickup

No booking fee and no pickup fee. Items come to me by route pickup, a drop-off, an approved shipping label, or an Uber courier — whatever keeps costs low before anything earns.

I do the work

I evaluate each piece, prep it when prep meaningfully helps, photograph it, list it, and route it to the platform where it's most likely to sell.

Paid when it sells

Resale is commission-based. You're paid your share of the net sale price once an item sells. The standard term is 180 days, with most strong inventory selling in roughly six to nine months.
