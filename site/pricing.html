Curated Resale + Consignment

Commission-based · agreement required.

No booking fee and no pickup fee. Commission is calculated on the net sale price, after platform fees, shipping, and approved transaction costs.

Bags made up primarily of lower-value items may need an adjusted commission because of the labor-to-value ratio. This is reviewed at intake and communicated before anything is listed. Standard resale term is 180 days; most strong inventory sells in roughly 6–9 months.

Good to know

How billing actually works.

Hourly and project pricing may include work between visits — communication, coordination, scheduling, resale routing, documentation, inventory, logistics, continuity, vendor coordination, shipping, and planning. The business is built around asynchronous, continuity-based workflows. Hourly and project work is invoiced directly, flex blocks are prepaid, commission payouts are monthly, and resale timelines vary by category.

Local LA-area travel is generally not billed separately; projects are routed for efficiency. Outside a reasonable range, a travel fee or project-integrated pricing applies based on travel time, logistics, and complexity. Multi-state work is project-scoped.
