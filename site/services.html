Resale & Consignment

Commission-based · agreement required

The resale pile untouched for years, handled properly. No booking fee, no pickup fee. Full commission detail is on the pricing page.

  • Furniture / item research — single item, one stop, one focused question (light research, quick review, drive time, communication). Multi-item and estate-level shifts into House Calls, Resets, or Legacy scope. ≈ $25

Items are pre-owned, estate, or vintage and may show wear consistent with category. Not certified appraisal or authentication unless separately arranged.
